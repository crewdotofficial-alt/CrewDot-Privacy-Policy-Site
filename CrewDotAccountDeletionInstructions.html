CrewDot Account Deletion Process

Thank you for choosing CrewDot. This page explains how to permanently delete your CrewDot account and all associated personal data in compliance with our privacy policies and Google Play requirements.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account through the CrewDot app by following these steps:

  • Log in to the CrewDot app using your registered email.
  • Navigate to Account Settings from Hamburger.
  • Select the Delete Account option.
  • Request a One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your registered email address.
  • Enter the OTP to verify your identity.
  • Confirm permanent deletion of your account and data.

Data Deleted Upon Account Removal

When you delete your account, the following data will be removed permanently:

  • Your user profile and personal information.
  • All data associated with your account in the CrewDot system.
  • Your subscription status and history. Please note that subscriptions will end immediately and no refunds are provided.

Data Retention & Legal Requirements

Certain data may be kept beyond deletion only as legally required or for compliance purposes. CrewDot does not retain or use your personal data for marketing after account deletion.

Support & Assistance

If you experience any issues deleting your account or have questions, please contact CrewDot Support at crewdotofficial@gmail.com.

Request Account Deletion via Email

Alternatively, you may request account deletion by emailing us. Please include your registered email and a clear request for account deletion.
